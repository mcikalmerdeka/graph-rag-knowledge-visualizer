# Elon Musk Profile

## Personal Information
- **Full Name:** Elon Reeve Musk
- **Birth Date:** June 28, 1971
- **Birth Place:** Pretoria, South Africa
- **Current Residence:** Austin, Texas, USA
- **Citizenship:** South Africa (birth), Canada (through mother), USA (naturalized)
- **Age:** 54 years old (as of 2025)
- **Net Worth:** $384-480 billion (2025) - World's wealthiest person
- **Marital Status:** Single (previously married to Justine Wilson, Talulah Riley, Grimes)
- **Children:** 12 children (including twins and triplets from first marriage, children with Grimes)

## Family Background
- **Mother:** Maye Musk - Canadian model and dietitian
- **Father:** Errol Musk - South African electromechanical engineer
- **Siblings:** Kimbal Musk (younger brother, entrepreneur), Tosca Musk (younger sister, filmmaker)
- **Childhood:** Showed early interest in technology, taught himself programming at age 10, created video game "Blastar" at age 12

## Education
- **Primary/Secondary:** Waterkloof House Preparatory School, Pretoria; Bryanston High School; Pretoria Boys High School
- **Undergraduate:** Queen's University, Kingston, Ontario, Canada (transferred)
- **Bachelor's:** University of Pennsylvania (1995) - dual degrees in Economics (Wharton) and Physics
- **Graduate:** Stanford University (PhD program in Physics) - dropped out after 2 days to pursue entrepreneurship

## Business Empire

**Tesla Inc. (2003-Present)**
- **Role:** CEO, Product Architect
- **Industry:** Electric vehicles, clean energy, autonomous driving
- **Products:** Model S, Model 3, Model X, Model Y, Cybertruck, Tesla Semi, Roadster
- **Energy:** Powerwall, Powerpack, Megapack, Solar Roof, solar panels
- **Autonomy:** Full Self-Driving (FSD) technology, Robotaxi service launched 2025
- **Innovation:** Supercharger network, 4680 battery cells, AI5 chips (40x faster than previous)
- **Market Cap:** $800+ billion (fluctuates with stock price)

**SpaceX (2002-Present)**
- **Role:** CEO, Chief Engineer
- **Industry:** Aerospace, space exploration, satellite internet
- **Achievements:** 
  - First private company to launch, orbit, and recover spacecraft (2010)
  - First private company to send astronauts to ISS (2020)
  - Reusable Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy rockets (547+ launches, 99.4% success rate)
  - Starship rocket development for Mars colonization
- **Starlink:** 7,000+ satellites in orbit, 5+ million subscribers, global broadband internet
- **Valuation:** $150+ billion (private company)
- **Mission:** Make humanity multi-planetary, establish Mars colony

**X Corp (formerly Twitter) (2022-Present)**
- **Acquisition:** $44 billion purchase completed October 2022
- **Rebranding:** Renamed to "X" in 2023 as part of "everything app" vision
- **Changes:** Significant staff reductions, modified content moderation, subscription features, long-form video
- **Vision:** Create comprehensive platform for social media, payments, and services

**Neuralink (2016-Present)**
- **Role:** Co-founder, CEO
- **Industry:** Brain-computer interfaces (BCI), neurotechnology
- **Technology:** Implantable brain chips for treating neurological conditions
- **Applications:** Paralysis treatment, vision restoration, cognitive enhancement
- **Status:** First human implant performed January 2024

**The Boring Company (2016-Present)**
- **Role:** Founder
- **Industry:** Tunnel construction, underground transportation
- **Projects:** Las Vegas Convention Center Loop, Vegas Loop expansion
- **Technology:** Tunnel-boring machines, autonomous underground transport
- **Goal:** Alleviate urban traffic congestion through underground networks

**xAI (2023-Present)**
- **Role:** Founder, CEO
- **Industry:** Artificial intelligence development
- **Mission:** "Understand the universe" - develop AI systems that can discover truth
- **Products:** Grok chatbot integrated with X platform
- **Philosophy:** Compete with OpenAI, focus on "maximally curious" AI

**Previous Ventures**
- **Zip2 (1995-1999):** Online business directory and mapping software. Sold to Compaq for $307 million.
- **X.com / PayPal (1999-2002):** Online payment company. Merged with Confinity, renamed PayPal. Sold to eBay for $1.5 billion. Musk earned $165 million.
- **OpenAI (2015-2018):** Co-founder and initial funder. Left board due to disagreements about direction. Attempted to acquire for $97.4 billion in 2025 (rejected).

## Recent Activities (2024-2025)

**Government Role:**
- Served as senior adviser to President Donald Trump (2025)
- Headed Department of Government Efficiency initiative
- Spearheaded federal cost-cutting efforts
- Stepped down from government role in May 2025

**Business Developments:**
- Tesla Robotaxi service launch with millions of app downloads
- SpaceX Starship successful satellite deployment (2025 milestone)
- FSD v14 development ongoing with AI5 chip integration
- xAI Grok integration with X platform
- Continued Starlink global expansion

**Recognition and Awards:**
- Time Magazine 100 Most Influential People (2025)
- Wash100 Award for technology and government efficiency contributions
- Named "Person of the Year" by Time Magazine (2021)
- Royal Aeronautical Society Gold Medal (2012)
- IEEE Honorary Membership (2015)

## Vision and Philosophy

**Core Missions:**
1. **Sustainable Energy:** Accelerate world's transition to sustainable energy through Tesla's EVs and energy products
2. **Multi-planetary Life:** Make humanity multi-planetary through SpaceX Mars colonization
3. **Safe AI:** Develop AI that benefits humanity while warning about existential risks
4. **Transportation Revolution:** Transform transportation through electric vehicles, autonomy, and underground systems
5. **Human Enhancement:** Merge human consciousness with AI through Neuralink

**Management Style:**
- Hands-on engineering involvement ("first principles" thinking)
- Extremely ambitious timelines (often missed but drives progress)
- Direct, sometimes controversial communication
- High-intensity work culture at his companies
- Rapid iteration and willingness to fail publicly

## Controversies and Challenges

**SEC and Regulatory Issues:**
- 2018 "funding secured" tweet about Tesla privatization - settled with SEC, paid $20 million fine, stepped down as Tesla chairman
- Ongoing tensions with SEC over social media use regarding Tesla

**Public Disputes:**
- Legal battles with Twitter over acquisition (pre-settlement)
- Public disagreements with other tech leaders (Bill Gates, Jeff Bezos)
- Controversial statements on various social and political issues
- Lawsuit with OpenAI over alleged mission betrayal

**Workplace Culture:**
- Reports of high-pressure environments at Tesla and SpaceX
- Allegations of workplace safety issues
- Mass layoffs at X/Twitter following acquisition

**Personal Life:**
- Complex relationships and multiple marriages
- Custody disputes with Grimes (Claire Boucher)
- Public disagreements with daughter Vivian Jenna Wilson (formerly Xavier)
- Concerns about work-life balance given leadership of multiple major companies

**Current Status:**
As of late 2025, Musk remains actively involved in leading Tesla, SpaceX, X, Neuralink, The Boring Company, and xAI simultaneously. His influence extends beyond business into technology policy, political discourse, and public conversations about AI safety, climate change, and space exploration. Despite controversies, his companies continue to push boundaries in their respective industries, with Tesla leading EV adoption, SpaceX dominating commercial space launch, and Starlink providing global internet connectivity.
